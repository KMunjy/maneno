Maneno — Privacy Policy

Effective: 10 June 2026 · Applies to the Maneno web app and its mobile (App Store / Google Play) editions.
The short version: Maneno collects no personal data, has no accounts, no ads, no trackers, and sends nothing to any server. Everything you do in the game stays on your device.

What we store — and where

Maneno stores the following only on your device (browser localStorage / app storage):

  • Your chosen display name (optional, free text you enter)
  • Game settings (difficulty, timer mode, sound, learn mode)
  • Play statistics (puzzles solved, streaks, best times, achievements, recent history)
  • An optional email address if you enter one at registration — it is saved locally and never transmitted

None of this leaves your device. We cannot see it, and neither can anyone else.

What we do NOT do

  • No analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising SDKs
  • No cookies for tracking
  • No account creation on a server; no passwords
  • No sale or sharing of data — we hold no data to sell
  • No collection from children or anyone else; the app is suitable for all ages

Offline use

Maneno is installable and works offline. The service worker caches the app's own files on your device for offline play; it does not transmit usage data.

Sharing

If you tap “Share result”, your device's native share sheet opens with a text summary you control. Nothing is shared unless you complete that action yourself.

Deleting your data

Clear the app's site data in your browser (or uninstall the app) and everything is gone. There is no server-side copy to request deletion of.

Changes

If a future version adds online features (e.g. optional cross-device sync), this policy will be updated first, and any data collection will be opt-in and disclosed in plain language.
