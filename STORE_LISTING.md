## Full description (≤4000 chars — both stores)
```
Maneno is the first crossword built for Bantu languages — Shona, Swahili and Zulu.

Most word games can't spell these languages. Maneno can. Letter-pairs like ng', zv, ch and dl each fill ONE square, exactly the way the language sounds. It's a small thing that changes everything — and no other crossword does it.

Play for the joy of a crossword in your own language, or switch on Learn Mode to study a mother tongue one word at a time.

• THREE LANGUAGES — Shona and Swahili, plus Zulu (Beta). Switch any time.
• ONE SOUND, ONE SQUARE — a grapheme-aware grid that respects each orthography.
• FOUR LEVELS — Learner, Intermediate, Experienced, Expert.
• LEARN MODE — see the English meaning and pronunciation of every word.
• THEMED CATEGORIES — family, animals, food, nature, places, body, time, values and more.
• A DAILY PUZZLE — the same grid for everyone, every day. Compare with friends and family.
• KEEP YOUR STREAK — achievements, best times, and a daily-play calendar.
• PLAYS OFFLINE — install it and play anywhere.
• NO ADS. NO TRACKING. NO ACCOUNT. Your data never leaves your device.

Made for the diaspora, for heritage learners, for parents passing a language to their kids — and for anyone who loves words.

"Maneno" is Swahili for "words."
```
