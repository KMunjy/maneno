A crossword that finally fits the language

Bantu languages are built on letter-pairs. Maneno treats them the way speakers do.

▦

One sound, one square

Digraphs like ng', zv, ch and dl each fill a single cell — the thing ordinary crosswords get wrong. Type them or tap the grapheme keypad.

◆

Play or learn

Switch on Learn Mode for English meanings and pronunciation, or just enjoy the puzzle. Built for the diaspora, heritage learners, and word lovers.

▲

Your level, your way

Beginner to advanced, themed categories (Family, Animals, Food…), a timer if you want it, and a streak to keep you coming back.

◷

A daily puzzle

One shared puzzle a day — the same grid for everyone, everywhere. Compare notes with friends and family.

✦

Three languages

Shona and Swahili today, Zulu in Beta, with more Bantu languages to come. The same engine, your orthography.

▢

Free & installable

Runs in any browser, works offline, and installs to your home screen like a native app. No sign-up required to start.

How it works

Pick a language & category

Shona, Swahili or Zulu — then a theme or the daily puzzle.

Fill the grid

Type on your keyboard or tap digraph keys. Letter-pairs snap into one square automatically.

Learn & track

See meanings in Learn Mode, build a streak, and beat your best time.
